what it is

most apps tell you what to do next. afterward works the other way around - you open it after you've done something, type whatever you did, and that's it. no format required. no daily streak. no reminders asking where you've been.

built for people who are neurodivergent, easily overwhelmed, or just done with the apps that make them feel behind. the language is calm. the design is quiet. it works when you do, and waits when you don't.
